UDAAN Technical Supervision

Nepal Unmanned Aircraft (Drone) Association — UDAAN was proud to technically supervise the recently concluded 10-day Drone Training organized by the Film Development Board.

Our role was to ensure that the 25 trainees received instruction aligned with professional standards and safety protocols. With expert trainers from Vayuratha (India), Drone Hub Nepal, and Nepal Police, this program marks a significant step in professionalizing drone use in the Nepali film industry.

Congratulations to the Board and all participants on a successful session!
