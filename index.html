The Challenge

Nepal's Drone Sector Needs a Unified Voice

Rapid UAV adoption across agriculture, disaster response, film, and logistics has outpaced regulation. Operators face unclear airspace rules, rising registration costs, and fragmented training standards.

Without a national body to bridge industry and government, innovation stalls and public trust erodes. UDAAN exists to close that gap.
