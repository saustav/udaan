Knowledge Base

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about RPAS operations and UDAAN membership in Nepal.

help

How do I apply for UDAAN membership?

Use the contact form on this page or email us directly. You must meet all eligibility criteria listed on our membership page.

badge

What are the eligibility requirements?

Applicants must be Nepali citizens aged 18+, mentally sound with no public property forfeitures, free of criminal or corruption convictions, and actively involved in drone ownership, operation, or training.

school

Does UDAAN provide pilot certification?

We provide specialized training modules and bridge courses aligned with CAAN regulatory standards to help you achieve your Remote Pilot License (RPL).

no_accounts

Are there no-fly zones in Kathmandu?

Yes — most of the valley is restricted due to TIA proximity. Contact us for guidance on permitted flight corridors and CAAN authorization requirements.

groups

Ready to Join Nepal's Drone Community?

Become a UDAAN member and gain access to regulatory support, training programs, and Nepal's largest network of UAV professionals.
